📚 Methodology Overview

🌍 Global Scenario Modeling

Our comprehensive systems model integrates macroeconomic variables, policy assumptions, and climate scenarios to create robust economic projections. The model uses dynamic equilibrium frameworks to capture complex interdependencies between fiscal policy, monetary policy, and environmental regulations.

🏢 Company Valuation Framework

Company valuations are derived using a multi-factor approach that combines traditional financial metrics with ESG performance indicators. The model employs Monte Carlo simulation to generate probability distributions of potential outcomes across different economic scenarios.

📊 Risk Assessment

Risk assessment incorporates both systematic and idiosyncratic factors, including climate transition risks, regulatory changes, and market volatility. Value-at-Risk calculations provide downside protection estimates at various confidence levels.

🔬 Technical Implementation

The model leverages advanced statistical techniques including regime-switching models, behavioral economics principles, network effects analysis, and machine learning algorithms for pattern recognition and forecasting.

📈 Validation & Calibration

Model parameters are calibrated using historical data and validated through backtesting across multiple market cycles. Regular updates ensure alignment with current market conditions and regulatory environments.
